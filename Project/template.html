Explanations - Opening an Isolated Control-Panel

This is a pattern for opening and closing a control-panel as in the case of svOptionsPanel directive displaying svOptions[visualizationType]. This is an isolated control-panel (not a general control-panel, because there can be many of these active at a time and you can look at visualization options for two charts simultaneously).

A control-panel may have a closed state and an open state. These two states can be expressed in a single boolean variable exposed in the scope, which we will call "show".
